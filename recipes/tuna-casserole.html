Easy Tuna Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 can tuna, drained
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the tuna, rice, peas, sour cream, cheese, and onion.
  3. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
