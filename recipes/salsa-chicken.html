Salsa Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the chicken strips until no longer pink.
  2. Add the salsa, cumin, and chili powder.
  3. Cook for an additional 5 minutes or until heated through.
