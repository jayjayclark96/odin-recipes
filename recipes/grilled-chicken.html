Grilled Chicken with Rosemary Bacon

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1/2 cup bacon, diced

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, garlic, and rosemary.
  3. Brush the chicken with the herb mixture and let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.
  5. Sprinkle the diced bacon over the chicken during the last few minutes of cooking.
