Crunchy French Onion Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, sauté the onions until caramelized.
  2. Add the chicken strips and cook until no longer pink.
  3. Stir in the garlic, paprika, and cumin.
  4. Cook for an additional 5 minutes or until heated through.
